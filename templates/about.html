About Us

Precision Engineering Excellence Since 1997

Nirmal Precision Pvt. Ltd., established in 1997 in Bhayandar, has been a trusted name in precision manufacturing. Strategically located near Mumbai, we benefit from excellent connectivity and logistics infrastructure.

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality stainless steel and steel fasteners, machined components from M4 to M36, complying with international DIN standards. Our precision-engineered products serve diverse industries worldwide.
